\# Hermes



Hermes is a local-first autonomous research and semantic memory system built around:



\- Obsidian

\- GPT synthesis

\- semantic concept extraction

\- autonomous research queues

\- living concept pages

\- knowledge dashboards



\## Features



\- Autonomous AI research

\- Defuddle article ingestion

\- Semantic memory

\- Persistent concept tracking

\- Obsidian wikilinking

\- Dashboard generation

\- Research queue orchestration



\## Architecture



Internet

↓

Hermes Research Agent

↓

Semantic Extraction

↓

Concept Memory

↓

Living Knowledge Pages

↓

Obsidian Vault
